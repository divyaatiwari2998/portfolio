Project Overview

What Was Built

The Eye Clinic Management System was a professional enterprise project developed during my Software Engineering role at Capgemini, Chennai. The system was designed to digitise and streamline core healthcare operations for an eye clinic — replacing manual, paper-based workflows with a data-driven application.

The project covered three core pillars: patient record management, appointment tracking, and operational reporting. The goal was to improve data accessibility for clinic staff, reduce appointment scheduling errors, and enable management to monitor clinic performance through structured reports.

I contributed across the development lifecycle — from translating business requirements into system features, to functional testing and data validation in an Agile sprint environment.

Key Features

System Capabilities

Appointment Tracking

Operational Reporting
Reporting dashboards surfacing key operational metrics — daily appointments, patient flow, and clinic utilisation data.

Functional testing and validation of all data entry flows to ensure system reliability and consistency across modules.
